What we provide

ELIXIR Ireland offers training in fundamental data science skills for life scientists, such as R analysis and data management planning. Our training primarily focuses on offering courses to over 100 PhD students within the Science Foundation Ireland (SFI) Centre for Research Training in Genomics Data Science program.
